Our Quality Promise

The Anatomy of Excellence.

The TRU-Core™ Foundation

Every TRU Vanity is built upon our signature TRU-Core™, an E0-grade Action TESA HDHMR board. This high-density foundation is engineered for the humidity of the Indian bathroom, providing exceptional resistance to moisture, warping, and termites.

  • ✓ Superior Moisture Resistance: Prevents swelling and delamination.
  • ✓ Zero Emission: E0-grade board for healthy indoor air quality.
  • ✓ Termite & Borer Proof: Built for a lifetime of service.

The Flawless Finish

We use durable 1mm HPL from global leaders like Merino and Greenlam for a surface that resists scratches and stains. This is sealed with waterproof PUR bonding and Rehau ABS edge banding, creating a monolithic panel that protects the core.

  • ✓ 1mm Thick HPL: Superior impact and scratch resistance.
  • ✓ PUR Waterproof Bonding: Creates a permanent, seamless, and waterproof edge.
  • ✓ Rehau ABS Edging: The global standard for durability and a perfect finish.

Refined Engineering

A luxury experience is in the details you feel every day. That's why we use only German-engineered soft-close hardware from brands like Hettich or Blum, and lead-free brass faucets with world-class Kerox or Sedal ceramic cartridges.

  • ✓ German Soft-Close Systems: Silent, effortless motion for doors and drawers.
  • ✓ Kerox/Sedal Cartridges: Guaranteed for a lifetime of smooth, drip-free operation.
  • ✓ Lead-Free Brass Faucets: Safe, durable, and beautifully finished.
